CAPA-Driven Reduction of PCBA Failures

In 2023, PCBA failure rate at final testing exceeded 9.5%, affecting delivery timelines and customer confidence. A cross-functional task force was deployed to analyze, mitigate and prevent defects at the root cause level.

Through a combination of Pareto, Fishbone, and 5 Whys, the main contributors were identified: soldering inconsistency, board warpage after reflow, and misalignment due to stencil wear. Data was confirmed via DOE and reflow profiling.

Implemented stencil replacement policy, reflow recipe tuning, operator retraining, and inline inspection triggers. CAPAs were validated through pilot production over 6 weeks with data tracked in JMP and Minitab.

Results: PCBA Failure Rate ↓ 32%, FPY ↑ 15%, Scrap cost ↓ €45.000. Monitoring plan sustained improvements for over 4 months. Project highlighted in corporate quality review as benchmark initiative.
